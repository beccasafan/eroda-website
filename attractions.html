Attractions

The Lighthouse Eroda's famous lighthouse is the perfect place to catch a beautiful sunset at night. Be sure to check out our Weather Channel for daily updates on Eroda's peculiar cloud coverage.

Deep Sea Diving School Eroda's Deep Sea Diving School is one of the top diving schools in the world. We have courses for all levels of divers. Erodians still use the peculiar old time tradition of learning to dive with rocks in their pockets. Think you've got what it takes? Come learn from the best diving instructors that the world has to offer.

Movie Theatre Showing a few peculiar flicks a night, Eroda's movie theatre is the perfect spot for all occasions! Check out our Check out a preview to see what is coming soon!

Eroda's Official Gift Shop Find the perfect memento at Eroda's Official Gift Shop. You'll find a peculiar assortment of fine gold earrings (an ancient Eroda traditional artifact), snacks, and merchandise. Bring something special home for the entire family!

Sea Rock Brewery Sample Eroda's finest selection of beers on tap at the Sea Rock Brewery. Grab a glass and fill it up, as you prepare to make memories while sipping Eroda's peculiar classic brews. Make sure to ask the local bartender to pour a glass of ale out to appease the peculiar Celtic water spirit Shenandoah.

The Fisherman's Pub Located on the corner of Cherry Street and Golden Way, The Fisherman's Pub serves up classic fare in a casual setting. Be sure to try their fish & chips and order a pint of your favourite local brew. The only rule of the bar? Don't mention a pig in the pub.

Sally's Tavern Casual bar eatery with a large selection of beers on tap and wines by the glass. If you have a strong stomach, be sure to ask for their peculiar home made hot sauce - it's been a staple at this restaurant for over four generations!

Flanagan Fish market Serving up the finest peculiar fish that Eroda has to offer, stop by the Flanagan Fish Market for the freshest catch of the day. The halibut (when available) is this fish market's specialty. Prices may vary based on quantity and quality.

Hiking trails The Historic Harbour Road Trail is one of Eroda's most popular hiking trails. People travel from near and far for the chance to complete this beautiful nature walk. We recommend starting at the north end of this peculiar trail and making your way to the south. The shortest trail takes no more than 45 minutes end to end, while the longer trail will take around 3 hours to complete.

Montgomery Maclean Castle One of the oldest, most peculiar castles in the region, Montgomery Maclean Castle is the premiere historical landmark of Eroda. This 13th Century relic is a must-see for all tourists looking to learn more about Eroda's rich history.

Maritime Museum Learn about Eroda's extensive Maritime history! Come visit and explore Eroda's peculiar collection of maritime vessels and artifacts. Be sure to book in your group's guided tour in advance, as they fill up quickly. Multi-language guide books are available onsite as well.

Kieran Studio Love to paint? Drop by Kieran's studio for a class today! She'll teach you the peculiar basics of drawing, sculpture, painting - watercolor & acrylic - and more! Whether you are a beginner or an experienced painter, Kieran has a class for you. Sign up for a class today!

Eroda Ferry Catch a ride to one of the peculiar neighboring islands or take a relaxing cruise and enjoy the abundant wildlife.
